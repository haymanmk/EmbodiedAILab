# Jiayuan Mao

## Overview

Jiayuan Mao is a robotics and AI researcher whose work centers on learning, reasoning, and planning with neuro-symbolic concepts. In [[Integrated Learning and Planning - Mao]], she frames general-purpose physical intelligence as a problem of learning compositional concepts — objects, relations, skills, rules, constraints, and action models — that can be grounded by neural networks and recombined by planners.

## Key facts

- Current role in the seminar description: Member of Technical Staff at Amazon Frontier AI & Robotics and incoming assistant professor at the University of Pennsylvania.
- PhD work: MIT CSAIL, advised by Joshua B. Tenenbaum and Leslie Pack Kaelbling.
- Research theme: decompose robot learning into a vocabulary of [[Neuro-Symbolic Concepts]] where symbolic structure supports composition and neural modules handle grounding.
- Systems mentioned in or adjacent to the seminar include PDSketch, MAGIC, SetItUp, STACK, and Retriever.
